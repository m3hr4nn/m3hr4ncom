Last updated: January 2025

Your privacy is important to me. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and protect your information when you visit my portfolio website at m3hr4n.com.

When you request my CV through the contact form, I collect:

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

When you visit my website, I may automatically collect:

  • Browser type and version

I use minimal cookies and local storage for:

2. How I Use Your Information

I use the collected information solely for:

  • Sending you my CV when requested

3. Information Sharing

I do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

I may share information only in these limited circumstances:

4. Data Security

I implement security measures to protect your information:

5. Data Retention

I retain your personal information for:
